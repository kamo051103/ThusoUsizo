Who We Are

TUCE

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment is a non-profit organisation created to serve the Yeoville and Bellevue communities in Johannesburg. Our work focuses on community social development, education support, information services and programmes that uplift children, youth, adults, elderly people and refugees.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, information resources, social development and support programmes that improve everyday lives.

Our Vision

To become a trusted community organisation that helps build informed, educated, supported and united communities across South Africa.

Our Programmes

Community Helpdesk

A community-based information resource and helpdesk centre for people who need guidance and support.

Homework Centre

Supporting school children with homework, learning assistance and educational encouragement.

Elderly Activities

Activity programmes and services that support elderly people and encourage active community participation.

Refugee Support

A language and cultural centre supporting refugees with integration, communication and belonging.

Community in Action

Our programmes are built around real people, real communities and real opportunities for growth.

Our Partnership

Yeoville Boys Primary

We are proud to have a partnership with Yeoville Boys Primary, supporting our goal of uplifting learners through education, development and community-based programmes.

Working Together

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment works with organisations in social development, education, arts, culture and related programmes to create long-term community impact.
