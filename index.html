Who We Are

TUCE

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment is a non-profit organisation serving the Yeoville and Bellevue communities in Johannesburg, with a vision to grow into other areas of South Africa. Our work focuses on community social development, education support, information services and programmes that uplift children, youth, adults, elderly people and refugees.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, information resources, social development and support programmes that improve everyday lives.

Our Vision

To become a trusted community organisation that helps build informed, educated, supported and united communities across South Africa.

Our Programmes

Community Helpdesk

Elderly Activities

Activity programmes and services that support elderly people and encourage active community participation.

Refugee Support

A language and cultural centre supporting refugees with integration, communication and belonging.
