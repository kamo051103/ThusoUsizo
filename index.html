About Us

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment is a non-profit organisation established to provide community social development and information services within the Yeoville and Bellevue communities of Johannesburg, with plans to expand our impact throughout South Africa.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, support services, information resources and development initiatives that improve the quality of life for children, youth, adults and senior citizens.

Our Programmes

Community Information Centre

Providing information resources and helpdesk services for community members.

Homework Centre

Supporting learners through educational assistance, mentorship and academic guidance.

Elderly Programmes

Creating activities and services that promote healthy and active ageing.

Language & Culture Centre

Supporting refugees through language development and cultural integration programmes.

Working Together

We collaborate with organisations involved in social development, education, arts, culture and community upliftment to create sustainable positive change.
