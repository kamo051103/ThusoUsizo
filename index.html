Empowering Communities Through Education and Support

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment is dedicated to creating opportunities, providing support services and strengthening communities throughout Johannesburg and beyond.

About Us

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment is a non-profit organisation established to provide community social development and information services within the Yeoville and Bellevue communities and, in the future, throughout South Africa.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, support programmes, social development initiatives and partnerships that improve the quality of life for children, youth, adults and senior citizens.

Our Programmes

Community Information Centre

Providing a community-based information resource and helpdesk centre.

Homework Centre

Supporting school children through educational assistance and learning opportunities.

Elderly Activity Programme

Creating programmes and services that promote active and healthy ageing.

Language and Cultural Centre

Supporting refugees through language and cultural integration initiatives.

Working Together

ThusoUsizo Community Empowerment collaborates with organisations involved in social development, education, arts, culture and related programmes to maximise positive impact within communities.
